# life-remaining

---

**What it is:**
A single-file web app that visualizes your entire life as a grid of weeks — showing exactly how much time you have lived and how much remains.

---

**How it works:**
You enter two things:
- Your **date of birth**
- Your **expected age at death** (default is 80 years)

The app instantly calculates and displays everything automatically.

---

**What it shows:**

| Stat | What it means |
|---|---|
| Weeks remaining | The big number at the top |
| Years remaining | Approximate years left |
| Months remaining | Approximate months left |
| Days remaining | Exact days left |
| Hours remaining | Exact hours left |
| Life elapsed % | How much of your life is gone |

---

**The grid:**
- Every single square = **1 week of your life**
- **Green squares** = weeks you have already lived
- **White square** = the current week you are in right now
- **Dark squares** = weeks still ahead of you

---

**Key features:**
- Saves your data automatically — remembers you every visit
- Updates itself every day at midnight
- Works in any browser
- Works as a live wallpaper in Lively Wallpaper
- Fullscreen mode
- Export as screenshot
- No internet required after first load
- No account, no tracking, completely private

---

**Design style:**
Pure black background, neon green terminal aesthetic, monospace font — inspired by hacker and cyberpunk interfaces. Clean, minimal, and intentionally confrontational — it is meant to make you think about how you spend your time.

---

**The idea behind it:**
Inspired by the concept of *memento mori* — the ancient reminder that life is finite. Seeing your life as a grid of small boxes makes the abstract idea of "time passing" feel very real and very visual.
